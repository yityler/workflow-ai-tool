## Overall Assessment

ChatGPT gives balanced, practical answers that would be easy to turn into product requirements or early design documentation. It is especially good at explaining the purpose of each concept in plain language and tying AI behavior back to user experience.

For the Workflow Generator Tool, ChatGPT's biggest strengths are clarity, product framing, and a strong sense of user control. Its answers emphasize not overwhelming the user, recommending actions rather than abstract analysis, and measuring success by workflow completion rather than AI engagement.

The main weakness is that the responses are less technically deep than DeepSeek's. They mention appropriate data structures and model components, but they do not go as far into real-time architecture, predictive modeling, latency management, or graph-based workflow logic.

## Score

**Overall score: 8.5/10**

ChatGPT appears strong for product strategy, UX design, and requirements writing for the Workflow Generator Tool. It would do well, but may need a more technical partner for detailed system architecture.
